Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Simply Resolve is designed to work without advertising tracking. This policy explains what information is handled and how.

Data you create in the app

Your resolutions, schedules, daily logs, and notes are stored on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, the data may be stored in your iCloud account and synced across your devices via Apple’s CloudKit.

Data collection

  • No sale of personal data.
  • No third-party ads tracking.
  • If crash reporting/diagnostics are enabled on your device, Apple may provide aggregated diagnostics.

iCloud / CloudKit

iCloud sync is handled by Apple. Your iCloud data is governed by Apple’s terms and privacy policy.

Support emails

If you contact support by email, your email provider will process the message. The website does not collect or store form submissions—contact buttons simply open your email app.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact support to request deletion of support correspondence.

Changes

This policy may be updated. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
