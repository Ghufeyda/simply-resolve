FAQ

Click a question to expand.

How do I mark a resolution as done or skipped?
Open the dashboard and swipe the card: right for done, left for skipped.
Can I edit past days?
Yes. Open a resolution’s review screen and select a day to edit its status and notes.
Where is my data stored?
Data is stored on your device. If iCloud is enabled, it can sync via CloudKit to your iCloud account.
I reinstalled the app. Can I restore my data?
If iCloud sync was enabled, your data should reappear after the app finishes syncing. If it wasn’t enabled, reinstalling may remove local-only data.
Does the app collect analytics or track me?
The app is designed to work without advertising tracking. See the Privacy Policy for specifics.
How do notifications work?
Notifications are optional and depend on your device settings. You can enable/disable them in iOS Settings.
